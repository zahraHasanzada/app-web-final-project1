The five major tribes of Afghanistan

Afghanistan is a country with a multinational and tribal society. The population of this country consists of several ethnic and linguistic groups, including: Pashtun, Tajik, Hazara, Uzbek, Imaq, Turkmen, Baloch, Pesha'i, Nuristani, Gujjar, Arab, Brahui, Qazalbash, Pamir, Kyrgyz, Sadat, Hindu, etc. The national anthem and the previous constitution of Afghanistan referred to these fourteen tribes
